Valyrian Vocal



About:

O' wanderer of the wild, who approaches this web app shall get his English turn into Valyrian; the language spoken in Old Valyria, the capital city of the once-great civilization known as the Valyrian Freehold situated in the fictional world created by Mr. George RR Martin in his popular TV Series 'Game Of Thrones' as well as book series 'A Song of Ice & Fire'.
